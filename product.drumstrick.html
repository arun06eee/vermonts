Drumstick - Moringa Oleifera

Moringa oleifera is the most widely cultivated species of the genus Moringa, which is the only genus in the family Moringaceae. Common names are Horseradish tree, Drumstick tree, Benzolive tree, Ben oil tree. It is extremely well-known veggie, cultivated in several regions of India. It got excellent therapeutic values and high nutrition content.
